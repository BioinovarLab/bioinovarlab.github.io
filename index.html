IoT-LAB provides a facility suitable for testing networking with small wireless sensor devices and heterogeneous communicating objects.

Open platform

The testbed is in open access. Don't wait anymore, create your IoT-LAB account.

Sign Up

Open Software

Build your application without any dependency requirement. Develop from scratch, or based on open-source libraries, or even with an OS.

Open Tools

Deploy and run your experiments with our webportal. Automate with the CLI tools or the Python client. Of course, you can make direct calls to our open API.
